I’m a passionate graphic designer based in Aotearoa, who traverses between the worlds of analogue and digital design. My strengths lie within typography and crafting bold and unconventional design solutions, as well as photography, which allows me to create independent bodies of work.

Through my research-led and exploratory design approach, I often enjoy coupling visual experiences with tactility while using music to inspire my designs. Emotion typically centres itself at the heart of my creations, while also taking concept-driven paths to create depth and meaning within my work. Through this, I aim to craft experimental and boundary-pushing designs that develop original and meaningful solutions, guided by curiosity, expression, and consideration.
