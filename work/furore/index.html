This playful, yet refined direction would help the brand to stand out against competitors and connect more deeply with the pressure-free needs of Aotearoa’s youth." - "Poster designs were centred around de-stigmatising loneliness and some of its associated causes to introduce and reinforce what the brand is all about, and aiming to make youth feel more comfortable, uplift them, boost their confidence and encourage them to step out and come along to a gig and meet new people." images: - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_03.jpg" - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_04.jpg" - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_05.jpg" - columns: - heading: "Outcome" paragraphs: - "This project illustrates the emphatic impact that music has on connecting people together by crafting inclusive and accessible spaces for young people to find lasting communities, combatting the youth loneliness epidemic in Aotearoa, one gig at a time." - heading: "" paragraphs: - "Through relatable and de-stigmatising messaging surrounding loneliness, Furore provides supportive spaces that foster confidence and connection, allowing youth to socialise in validating and non-judgemental environments across the country." images: - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_06.jpg" - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_07.jpg" - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_08.mp4" - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_09.jpg" - "/assets/images/work/case_studies/furore/furore_casestudy_10.jpg" --- {% include hero.html img=page.hero_image %}
